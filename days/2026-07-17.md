### Embedded AI systems

- **GigaWorld-Policy-0.5 is the embedded story of the window.** GigaAI's enhanced world action model keeps the training benefit of modeling future scene evolution (dense physical supervision for action generation) but strips video generation out of the inference path entirely. Action-only decoding through a Mixture-of-Transformers architecture lands at 85 ms latency on a local RTX 4090, and an agent-based AutoResearch pipeline searched the training configurations instead of humans. Every architecture choice here is about making world-model policies deployable on hardware a robot can actually carry. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13960)
- **Huawei's Atlas 950 physical unit makes its global debut at WAIC as the industry's largest-scale supernode, in a show carrying 108 chips on display.** At the Zhangjiang venue, more than ten general-purpose compute-chip companies exhibit side by side, with mainstream domestic GPU vendors nearly all on site and four emerging architectures competing in the same arena. The sovereign compute stack is not a slide anymore, it is a hall. [Global Times](https://www.globaltimes.cn/page/202607/1365377.shtml), [EqualOcean](https://equalocean.com/event/2026071422027-waic-2026-guide-ai-partnership-brighter-future)
