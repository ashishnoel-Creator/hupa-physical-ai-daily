### Research papers worth a look

- **TACO: TActile World Model as a Self-COrrector for Scalable VLA Post-Training (arXiv 2607.02840).** Uses a tactile world model to self-correct a VLA policy during post-training, so contact-rich failures get caught by predicted touch rather than by a human grader. Touch keeps moving from sensor demo to training-loop infrastructure, and every step in that direction adds a modality our capture spec eventually has to carry. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02840)
- **Cortex: A Bidirectionally Aligned Embodied Agent Framework for Long-horizon Manipulation (arXiv 2607.05377).** Aligns high-level agentic planning and low-level control in both directions for long-horizon tasks. Long-horizon is where demonstration data gets expensive, so frameworks that stretch it further per demo bend the data economics buyers care about. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05377)
- **ABot-M0.5: Unified Mobility-and-Manipulation World Action Model (arXiv 2607.00678).** A world action model unifying navigation and manipulation through alignment at three levels: temporal granularity, action space, and train-test consistency. The mobility-plus-manipulation merge is the same direction Xiaomi-Robotics-1 claims, arriving from the world-model side. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.00678)
- **Catch-up, never covered here: Qwen-VLA: Unifying Vision-Language-Action Modeling across Tasks, Environments, and Robot Embodiments (arXiv 2605.30280, submitted May 28; Alibaba Qwen).** A single VLA jointly pretrained on robot trajectories, human egocentric demonstrations, simulation, navigation and auxiliary vision-language data. Human ego demonstrations sitting inside a top-tier lab's unified pretraining mix, named as a first-class data source, is the demand signal for our exact product. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30280)
- **Catch-up, never covered here: World-VLA-Loop: Closed-Loop Learning of Video World Model and VLA Policy (arXiv 2602.06508, February, trending on Hugging Face this week).** Jointly refines an action-conditioned video world model and the VLA policy in a closed loop, using the world model as a virtual environment to avoid expensive real-world RL rollouts. The cheaper virtual rollouts get, the more the residual value concentrates in the real-world data that grounds the world model. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.06508)
