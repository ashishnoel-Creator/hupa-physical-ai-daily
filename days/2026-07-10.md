### Data collection

- **Orbbec's body-less capture platform is the capture signal of the day, and it moves the QUANXTA pattern one layer down the stack.** Where X Square packaged capture-plus-validation as a data company's product on July 6, Orbbec is a components giant selling the rig itself: EGO for first-person observation, UMI for gripper-interface capture, WristCam for hand-object interaction detail, offered as standardized products plus CM and JDM services to embodied model developers, robot makers, data acquisition service providers and data operators. The EGO RGB-D unit does chip-level depth direct output on Orbbec's MX6800 and then runs Ant Lingbo's LingBot-Depth 2.0 at capture time to fill missing depth and refine edges on reflective, transparent and occluded surfaces. The commercial context is the tell: Orbbec says humanoid-related orders have grown over 60% quarter-on-quarter since the start of 2025 and doubled again in Q1 2026. When anyone can buy a validated-spec ego rig from the same vendor that supplies the top humanoid OEMs, the rig stops being a moat. What is left is what the rig cannot ship with, the environments, the operating discipline and the audit trail on what was actually captured. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/orbbec-launches-hardware-platform-for-body-less-data-collection-2074842103866507265)
- **EgoWAM answers the question underneath the whole ego-data trade: what do you have to predict for in-the-wild egocentric human data to actually transfer to robots.** The Georgia Tech group's framing is exactly the industry problem, behavior cloning on human video entangles the transferable stuff (objects, scenes, task semantics) with the non-transferable stuff (human morphology, head motion, behavioral style). Their answer is world-action co-training with the right world target: raw pixel prediction transfers weakly, DINO features improve out-of-distribution generalization up to 4x, and 3D motion flow lifts in-domain performance 20-30% across real bimanual tasks. For anyone selling ego capture, this is the buyer-side recipe for extracting value from it, and it says the raw video is worth more when the capture preserves clean geometry and motion, which is a spec question. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08436)

### Embedded AI systems

- **LingBot-VA 2.0's inference design is the embedded read of the day's big model release.** Ant's new video-action foundation model is built from scratch for embodiment rather than adapted from a content-generation video model, and two of its four design choices are about running in the real world: a sparse MoE backbone to grow capacity without paying for it at inference, and an asynchronous scheme that predicts future latents in parallel with action execution, re-grounding each rollout on the latest observation. Real-time closed-loop control from a video-action model is the requirement that keeps pushing these architectures toward edge-friendly shapes. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08639)
- **Onboard VLMs for multi-robot control got a clean feasibility paper in the same window, running language-model-driven robotic control fully on-device across multiple agents.** Small signal, same direction: the policy is migrating onto the robot. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07403)

### Robotics & humanoids

- **The Nature surgery result is the humanoid capability story of the week, and the detail worth staring at is the hardware choice.** UCSD's team, led by Michael Yip with surgeons from the UCSD School of Medicine, did not use a surgical robot. They used stock Unitree G1 humanoids, teleoperated through motion capture, foot pedals and tracking systems, coordinating both arms to hold tissue with one hand and cut with the other. Seven procedures on large non-primate animal models, from physical exams to a successful gallbladder removal by a human-robot team, plus one surgery executed by a robot-robot pair. The framing in the accompanying Robot Report piece is that versatile humanoids, not another da Vinci, are the next surgical platform because one body can amortize across the whole hospital workflow. Two reads. Capability: commodity Chinese hardware is now precise enough for surgery when a human supplies the policy. Data: every hour of that teleoperation is exactly the high-stakes, expert-labeled demonstration data the VLA labs cannot get from warehouses, and hospitals are where it gets made. [UC San Diego](https://today.ucsd.edu/story/surgeons-use-teleoperated-humanoid-robots-to-perform-live-surgery-a-world-first), [The Robot Report](https://www.therobotreport.com/beyond-da-vinci-why-versatile-humanoid-robots-are-next-frontier-surgery/), [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07972)

### Research papers worth a look

- **EgoWAM: World Action Models Beyond Pixels with In-the-Wild Egocentric Human Data (arXiv 2607.08436, July 8). the ego-transfer recipe paper.** Controlled study of what world-prediction target makes egocentric human data transfer to robots: pixels transfer weakly, DINO features give up to 4x out-of-distribution gains, 3D motion flow gives 20-30% in-domain. World-action co-training scales better with in-the-wild human video than behavior cloning. The buyer-side manual for the data HuPa collects. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08436)
- **LingBot-VA 2.0 / Native Video-Action Pretraining for Generalizable Robot Control (arXiv 2607.08639, July 9). Ant's from-scratch video-action foundation model.** Semantic visual-action tokenizer, causal pretraining from scratch to dodge catastrophic forgetting, sparse MoE, asynchronous real-time inference. Few-shot generalization validated on real hardware. Read with the Orbbec item, the same group now touches capture and policy. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08639)
- **In vivo feasibility study of humanoid robots in surgery (arXiv 2607.07972, Nature, July 8). the UCSD teleoperated-surgery result.** Stock Unitree G1s, motion-capture teleop, seven procedures including a gallbladder removal on a live animal model. The reference point for how far commodity humanoid hardware goes when the policy is a human expert. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07972)
- **WAM-TTT: Steering World-Action Models by Watching Human Play at Test Time (arXiv 2607.06988, July 8). human video as a test-time control signal.** Steers a world-action model by watching human play, no retraining. Another entry in the week's theme of human data doing work beyond pretraining. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06988)
- **TouchWorld: A Predictive and Reactive Tactile Foundation Model for Dexterous Manipulation (arXiv 2607.07287, July 8).** A tactile foundation model for dexterous manipulation, predictive and reactive. Tactile is the modality every capture spec ignores and every dexterity result keeps needing. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07287)
- **EmbodiedGen V2: An Agentic, Simulation-Ready 3D World Engine for Embodied AI (arXiv 2607.07459, July 8).** Agentic scene generation for simulation-ready 3D worlds. The sim side keeps industrializing in parallel with real capture. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07459)
