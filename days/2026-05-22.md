### Data collection

- **Cortex AI (YC F25) is positioning as the egocentric-workplace data company.** Hand and body pose, depth, subtask labels, multi-industry workplace coverage. Yet another team writing the SOW we should be writing. Worth a direct intro call to scope where their capture is thin (industrial vs household vs hospital). [Cortex AI](https://cortexrobot.ai/ego/), [YC profile](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/cortex-ai)
- **Genesis AI's "$200 glove" model is now the new template.** GENE-26.5 launch (May 6) showed a 1:1:1 mapping from human hand to glove to robotic hand, with Genesis claiming 100x cheaper than teleop rigs and 5x more usable data per hour. Combined with Sunday's Skill Capture Glove (March), the gloves-not-headsets school is now well funded. The capture-modality battle is splitting cleanly into glove vs head-mount. Our wedge case for head-mount is the workplace and outdoor context the glove cannot give. [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/06/khosla-backed-robotics-startup-genesis-ai-has-gone-full-stack-demo-shows/), [Sunday — Memo](https://www.humanoidsdaily.com/news/sunday-unveils-memo-a-wheeled-domestic-robot-that-learns-from-200-gloves)
- **EgoVerse dataset reconfirmed (April 2026).** ~1,400 hours of in-the-wild manipulation across ~2,000 tasks, custom stereoscopic head rigs at 1920x1200 30fps, plus depth + IMU SLAM for 6DoF head pose. Joint output of Georgia Tech, Stanford, UCSD, ETH, MIT, Meta Reality Labs, Mecka AI and Scale AI. This is the academic anchor everyone in the head-mount camp is benchmarking against. [Labellerr writeup](https://www.labellerr.com/blog/egoverse-egocentric-robot-learning-dataset/), [arXiv 2604.07607](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.07607)
