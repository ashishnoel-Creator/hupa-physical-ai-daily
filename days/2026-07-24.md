### Data collection

- **EgoRecovery (arXiv 2607.19745, submitted July 22) put a number on a collection niche nobody prices separately: failure recovery data.** By arranging task-level failure configurations and recording short human recovery segments, operators generate more than 10x as much valid recovery data per hour as robot teleoperation. Recovery demonstrations are the clips a QC pipeline usually rejects as failed takes. This paper says they are a product, and that humans capture them an order of magnitude cheaper than robots do. A capture spec that tags failure-and-recovery segments instead of discarding them just became more valuable. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19745)
- **MEVION (arXiv 2607.17970) is a low-cost open-source data collection system for powerful, high-speed dual-arm manipulation.** Another entry in the cheap-capture-hardware lineage that runs from UMI grippers to Xiaomi's 100K hours. The collection rig keeps getting cheaper and more open, which moves the differentiation to operations and validation, where it already was. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17970)
- **Catch-up, never covered here: Unidata launched a multimodal egocentric capture system for humanoid training data on July 3.** One session records synchronized stereo video, full-body skeletal motion, continuous camera pose and per-frame depth from the stereo footage. Their pitch names the gap we keep pointing at: almost all existing ego footage is 2D RGB with no depth and no camera-pose metadata, so it cannot be reliably adapted across perception stacks or reconstructed in 3D. Another vendor building toward the full-sensor capture spec, which reads as validation of the rig-first approach and as a competitor entering the same lane. [KDH News](https://kdhnews.com/online_features/press_releases/unidata-launches-egocentric-data-system-to-train-humanoid-robots/article_ce8e35a3-a7b0-59ad-bebe-053a95823d63.html)

### Data annotation & validation

- **The China dataset quality standard is the validation story of the month, maybe the year.** The approved project, "Humanoid Robot Dataset Part 6: Quality Evaluation," is led by SAC/TC591 with drafting by the Beijing Machinery Industry Automation Research Institute and UBTech. It standardizes five things: data reliability, multi-modal alignment, task coverage, annotation reviewability, and evaluation comparability, across the full lifecycle from collection to acceptance. The stated goal is shifting the industry from quantity to quality and cutting invalid data and rework. Read what this does to the market: audit-trailed QC stops being a vendor differentiator and becomes a procurement requirement, with third-party evaluation written into the standard. Every data supplier selling into China will be graded against this. Buyers everywhere else will borrow the rubric. We have been building to exactly this bar since day one, and the largest humanoid market on earth just wrote it into policy. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/chinas-first-humanoid-robot-dataset-quality-evaluation-standard-project-approved-2079930360937811969)

### Research papers worth a look

- **EgoRecovery: Acquiring Failure Recovery Ability Through Human Recovery Demonstration (arXiv 2607.19745, submitted July 22).** Full read in the data collection section above. Human recovery demonstrations yield 10x more valid recovery data per hour than robot teleoperation. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19745)
- **MEVION: Low-Cost Open-Source Data Collection System for Powerful and High-Speed Dual-Arm Manipulation (arXiv 2607.17970).** Open hardware for dual-arm capture at speed and force levels handheld grippers do not reach. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17970)
- **Catch-up, never covered here: T-Rex: Tactile-Reactive Dexterous Manipulation (arXiv 2606.17055; Berkeley, NVIDIA, Stanford).** A Mixture-of-Transformer-Experts VLA that splits low-frequency visuomotor planning from high-frequency tactile refinement, beating dexterous and tactile-aware baselines by an average of 30% across 12 real-world tasks. The part that matters for us: it ships a fully open 100-hour tactile-synchronized dataset with 7,700+ trajectories and 200+ objects, and the recipe leans on human-video pretraining. Tactile-synchronized capture keeps moving from exotic to expected. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17055)
- **Catch-up, never covered here: UniDex: A Robot Foundation Suite for Universal Dexterous Hand Control from Egocentric Human Videos (arXiv 2603.22264, CVPR 2026).** A pipeline from egocentric human videos to 10M paired image-pointcloud-action frames and 50K+ trajectories across eight dexterous hands (6 to 24 DoFs), unified through a Function-Actuator-Aligned action space, with one VLA policy controlling all of them. Fully open-sourced. Ego human video as the upstream source for every hand embodiment at once is the strongest version yet of the argument that human capture amortizes across robot platforms. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22264)
