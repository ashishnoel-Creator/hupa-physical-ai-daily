    Data collection

    • Google DeepMind opened Street View grounding inside Project Genie globally on May 19. Genie 3 already powers a Waymo simulator for training the AV driver on edge cases that would be dangerous, illegal or impossible to stage on real roads (tornadoes, animals crossing highways, simultaneous equipment failures). Street View grounding extends that to specific real-world locations. The read for HuPa: the synthetic-data lane just got a serious new entrant on the geography side, and any pitch we make for real captured workplace data has to be clear about why a real capture beats a Genie 3 render of the same location. The honest answer is “physics, materials, lighting under specific human action” - none of which Genie 3 simulates. We need that on a slide. TechCrunch, Google blog
    • EgoExo-WM (arXiv 2605.15477, May 14). Extracts structured body pose from exocentric (third-person) video and transforms it to egocentric (first-person) video, unlocking the integration of in-the-wild exocentric data for egocentric world model training. Direct counter-thesis to the head-mount-only data thesis. If the exo-to-ego conversion holds up under benchmark, the data competition gets harder for our wedge. Read in detail. arXiv

    Research papers worth a look

    • Pelican-Unified 1.0 (arXiv 2605.15153, May 14). First embodied foundation model trained under a strict unification principle. Single VLM as understanding + reasoning module, plus a Unified Future Generator that jointly denoises future video and future actions through two modality-specific output heads in the same denoising process. Language, video, and action losses all backprop into the shared representation. Read against the modular VLA stack (PI’s pi-0.7, Figure’s Helix, NVIDIA’s GR00T) which keep these layers separate. arXiv
    • EgoExo-WM (arXiv 2605.15477, May 14). Extracts structured body pose from exocentric video and transforms it to egocentric video. The data argument: any third-person video (which is most of the internet) becomes training fuel for an egocentric world model. Direct counter to the head-mount-only data thesis. Read the failure-mode section before the next investor pitch on rig spec. arXiv
    • Being-H0.7 (arXiv 2605.00078, April 30). Latent world-action model pretrained on large-scale egocentric videos, claims strong cross-task performance. Companion to EgoExo-WM in the broader “egocentric video as the new ImageNet” thesis. arXiv
    • SAE World Congress 2026 - Embodied AI in Action white paper (arXiv 2605.10653). Cross-industry panel on lifecycle governance, human-centered design and standards for embodied AI in automotive contexts. The “where is the governance framework” question now has a real industry document attached. Worth skimming for OEM-facing pitches. arXiv
