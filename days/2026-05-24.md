### Research papers worth a look

- **Action with Visual Primitives (arXiv 2605.22183).** Published May 21. Frames manipulation as composition over a learned visual primitive set, rather than direct action prediction. Worth reading as an alternative to the dense-action-decoding path the headline VLAs (pi-0.7, Helix-02) are on. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/html/2605.22183v1)
- **Behavior Cloning for Active Perception with Low-Resolution Egocentric Vision (arXiv 2605.14106).** Active-perception task where a wrist-mounted egocentric RGB camera has to reposition to center a partially visible plant before grasp. Cheap rig, cheap camera, useful failure-mode reference for low-res egocentric capture. Read the resolution-vs-success-rate plots. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14106)
- **Multimodal fusion with VLA models for robotic manipulation (Information Fusion, Vol 129, May 2026).** Systematic review of 100+ VLA architectures. Finding: diffusion-based decoders + hierarchical fusion improve manipulation success across the benchmark suite. Best one-doc reference if anyone on the team needs to get current on the VLA modeling frontier. [ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1566253525011248)
