### Data collection

- **The PNDbotics-BeingBeyond deal is the first partnership this column has logged where the robot's stated commercial identity is the capture instrument.** Adam-U is described as a platform for embodied data acquisition and grasping training, offered with "exceptional openness" to third-party embodied models, and the partnership's roadmap names multi-scenario dataset construction alongside edge-model optimization and commercial benchmark projects. The QUANXTA and Orbbec moves from earlier this month commoditized the ego rig; this one commoditizes the robot-embodied capture cell. Each step down that path moves the differentiation further away from hardware and toward the two things a catalog cannot ship, the environments the platform gets deployed into and the discipline of what gets captured there. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/pndbotics-beingbeyond-form-strategic-partnership-2075394296059318272)
- **Insta360 is entering robotics, and the direction of travel matters: the capture company is becoming the robot company, not the reverse.** On its 11th anniversary the firm that holds over 40% of the global consumer 360-degree camera market formed a robotics team of nearly 100, pulled from DJI, Ecovacs and Horizon Robotics, to build an autonomous home camera robot codenamed Cameraman, first launch H2 2027. Founder Liu Jingkang's framing is exactly the capture-to-embodiment arc: a decade making cameras "see more fully," the next step making them "move on their own." A camera giant treating a robot as the natural next form factor for its imaging stack is one more sign that capture hardware and robotics are converging into one supply chain, and that supply chain is overwhelmingly Shenzhen's. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/insta360-marks-11th-anniversary-announces-entry-into-the-robotics-sector-2075589532719042561)
- **AnyDexRT from Cewu Lu's SJTU group attacks the least glamorous cost in teleop capture: retargeting.** Existing pipelines need hand-crafted objectives, per-hand calibration, or global shape matching, all of which make demonstrations expensive to collect across different dexterous hands. AnyDexRT does calibration-free retargeting via self-supervised fingertip correspondence plus a few human-guided anchors and a pinch contact classifier, and shows it working across diverse hands in real teleoperation. Cheaper, hand-agnostic retargeting lowers the unit cost of every teleoperated demonstration hour. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08341)

### Data annotation & validation

- **DexVerse landed as the second big evaluation harness in a week, and its baseline numbers are the useful part.** The benchmark spans 100 dexterous manipulation tasks across 3 arms and 6 hands, ships 3,180 teleoperated demonstrations with synchronized RGB, depth, point-cloud and proprioceptive streams, and provides configurable visual variation for texture, lighting and viewpoint. Benchmarked against it, Diffusion Policy, DP3, OpenVLA and π0.5 all show what the authors call substantial challenges in task generalization and visuomotor robustness. Read with RoboDojo from two days ago: evaluation infrastructure is standardizing fast, and every standardized eval makes "our policy works" a checkable claim, which pulls the whole market toward graded, provenance-clear data. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08751)

### Robotics & humanoids

- **The 1X hand release is the sharpest western dexterity signal of the week, and the design choices are worth reading closely.** Up to 25 DoF (22 actuated in fingers and palm plus the wrist), tendon-driven with motors housed in the forearm to keep fingers low-inertia, gear ratios of 5:1 to 15:1 so impacts backdrive the fingers instead of breaking them, IP68 and food-safe, 3.5 Nm at the thumb and 45 Nm distal flexion. The data-relevant part: high-resolution fingertip sensing for normal force, contact location and shear, integrated tightly with the 1XWM video-to-action model. Tactile is becoming a first-class training modality on a consumer-priced ($20K, or $499/month) robot, which raises the bar for what "complete" demonstration data means. The parallel CFO appointment of Bill Nash, who did capital raises at Cruise and Ursa Major, says the consumer shipment ramp is real enough to need that resume. [Humanoids Daily](https://www.humanoidsdaily.com/news/the-final-boss-of-home-robotics-1x-unveils-next-gen-robotic-hands-for-neo-alongside-new-cfo), [1X](https://www.1x.tech/discover/neos-hands)

### Research papers worth a look

- **DexVerse: A Modular Benchmark for Multi-Task, Multi-Embodiment Dexterous Manipulation (arXiv 2607.08751, July 9; Berkeley/HKU, Tomizuka and Yi Ma among authors). the week's second evaluation harness.** 100 tasks, 3 arms, 6 dexterous hands, 3,180 synchronized-modality demonstrations, configurable visual variation, VR teleop interface. Baselines including OpenVLA and π0.5 struggle on task generalization and visuomotor robustness. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08751)
- **Understanding and Mitigating the Video-Action Generalization Gap via Temporal Ratio (arXiv 2607.08127, July 9; Georgia Tech/MIT, Danfei Xu and Jiayuan Mao). why video-action models lose their video priors.** Video foundation models carry strong compositional priors but VAMs lose them after finetuning on robot action data. The paper's Temporal Ratio measures how much the action head leans on future latent rollouts versus the current frame, predicts compositional generalization from it, and fixes the gap at inference time with adaptive guidance. Directly relevant to anyone selling video data into VAM training. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08127)
- **AnyDexRT: Calibration-Free Dexterous Hand Retargeting with Few-Shot Human Guidance (arXiv 2607.08341, July 9; SJTU, Cewu Lu).** Self-supervised fingertip correspondence plus few-shot human anchors, no calibration, works across dissimilar dexterous hands. Lowers the cost floor of teleop demonstration collection. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08341)
- **ContactMimic: Humanoid Object Interaction via Contact Control (arXiv 2607.08742, July 9; UIUC, Saurabh Gupta group).** Humanoid-object interaction driven through explicit contact control. Contact-rich interaction is the data regime where clean demonstrations are scarcest. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08742)
- **Latent Memory Palace: Reasoning for Control as Autoregressive Variational Inference (arXiv 2607.08724, July 9; TRI and UW, Abhishek Gupta).** Frames reasoning-for-control as autoregressive variational inference, from the Toyota Research Institute side of the VLA world. Worth a read for where the reasoning-augmented control line is heading. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08724)
