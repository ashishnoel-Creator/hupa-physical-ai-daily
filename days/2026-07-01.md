### Data collection

- **TrainThemAI is the capture signal of the day, and it matters because it is HuPa's exact shape built somewhere else.** The company collects egocentric first-person video at scale from distributed operators recording household, kitchen and workspace activity on their phones, and separately runs teleoperation sessions on portable Quest 3 plus GELLO rigs, delivering policy-ready datasets in LeRobot, RLDS or HDF5 in weeks. It says its operator network is 270-plus people across Latin America, with capture also in the Philippines. The headline artefact is an ego-plus-wearable dataset dated June 2026: first-person video paired with hand pose and wrist trajectory tracking, pitched as action-labeled data at about one-tenth the cost of robot teleoperation, fully rights-cleared and MIT-licensed, with FHD sample clips posted on HuggingFace as a public shopfront. Read against the thesis, three things stand out. The capture-plus-bundled-annotation model is not unique to India, someone is already running it commercially from LatAm and SEA. The wrist-and-hand-pose wearable is the same instrumentation bet, recovering action labels from human demos without a robot in the loop. And the moat is not the method, which they are advertising openly, it is distribution and audit. Their hands are in Latin American kitchens and Filipino homes, not Indian hospitals, F&B floors and factories, and no public spec or reject-rate audit trail ships with the free samples. The environments they cannot reach are exactly the gap. [TrainThemAI](https://www.trainthemai.com/), [dataset on HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/TrainThemAI/POV-Egocentric-Video-Robotics-FHD-Samples)

### Data annotation & validation

- **The validation read is inside the same TrainThemAI listing, and it is a real bundled-annotation offer, not a capture-only one.** The delivered datasets carry episode-level language instructions, subtask segmentation, success and failure labels, contact-state tags and trajectory quality scores. That is capture and annotation sold as one integrated product, the same collapse of the two layers the China column keeps surfacing from the buyer side, here offered by a pure data vendor. The piece still missing from the public material is the audit half. There is a trajectory quality score but no published spec, no stated reject rate, no external QC benchmark to check the score against. A quality number you generate yourself and grade yourself is the exact place the audited-capture argument lives, and it is unfilled here. [TrainThemAI](https://www.trainthemai.com/)
