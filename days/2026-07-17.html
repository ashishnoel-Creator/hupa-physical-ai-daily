    Embedded AI systems

    • GigaWorld-Policy-0.5 is the embedded story of the window. GigaAI’s enhanced world action model keeps the training benefit of modeling future scene evolution (dense physical supervision for action generation) but strips video generation out of the inference path entirely. Action-only decoding through a Mixture-of-Transformers architecture lands at 85 ms latency on a local RTX 4090, and an agent-based AutoResearch pipeline searched the training configurations instead of humans. Every architecture choice here is about making world-model policies deployable on hardware a robot can actually carry. arXiv
    • Huawei’s Atlas 950 physical unit makes its global debut at WAIC as the industry’s largest-scale supernode, in a show carrying 108 chips on display. At the Zhangjiang venue, more than ten general-purpose compute-chip companies exhibit side by side, with mainstream domestic GPU vendors nearly all on site and four emerging architectures competing in the same arena. The sovereign compute stack is not a slide anymore, it is a hall. Global Times, EqualOcean

    India & SEA spotlight

    • The freshest Indian signal in a week is a profile with real numbers: Kerala’s iHUB Robotics builds humanoids full-stack in Kochi and is already exporting. Next on its list: India’s largest humanoid manufacturing facility, an industrial-humanoid R&D centre in Bengaluru, and 150 hires over two years. YourStory
