### Data collection

- **Lightwheel showed its full Physical AI data and evaluation stack at WAIC, and one of the four components is a human behavior data product.** The Real2Sim2Real loop: SimFoundry (simulation), EgoSuite (human ego data: real-world observations, operations, corrections and long-horizon task execution, cross-embodiment), RoboFinals (evaluation) and RoboStack (deployment). This is the company that booked $100M in Q1 orders, and it is packaging ego capture as one layer of a continuous learning loop rather than a standalone corpus. The closest thing yet to a productized version of the capture-plus-validation pipeline we run. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/market-industry/waic-2026-lightwheel-demonstrates-physical-ai-infrastructure-2078079009186357249), [Lightwheel](https://lightwheel.ai/)
- **EgoHTR open-sourced a capture pipeline that pairs egocentric wearables with a portable 3D scanner to produce scene-aligned 4D human motion.** 55 sequences, 150k+ frames of human terrain traversal in complex environments, from ETH Zurich, Stanford, Berkeley and TUM. The spec direction matters for anyone building rigs: ego video alone is no longer the deliverable, ego video registered against a scanned scene is. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13472)

### Robotics & humanoids

- **Sunday Robotics' ACT-2 Preview is the model release of the month for home robots.** The claims: 99.1% success on 778 of 785 autonomous laundry folds across unfamiliar households, zero-shot generalization to unseen homes and garments, and one fine-tuning example to teach a new behavior that then generalizes. Tony Zhao told Business Insider 2026 is when the autonomous home robot timeline "shrinks in a very drastic way." The skeptic's question is task breadth, laundry folding is one task family. But nobody else has published a 785-rollout evaluation in the wild. [Sunday Robotics](https://www.sunday.ai/blog/act-2-preview), [Techiexpert](https://techiexpert.com/sunday-robotics-debuts-act-2-preview-achieving-99-1-reliability-for-in-home-ai-robots/)
- **The PsiBot, YOFC and Changzhi optical module deployment is the sharpest single data point in the robots-do-shift-work arc.** Multiple embodied robots run the entire high-end optical module workflow autonomously: insertion, inspection, quality sorting, packaging. Psi-R2 combines sub-millimeter positioning with long-horizon task planning, plans grasp paths that avoid pin damage, and the projected ~10% cut in production loss over a 3-5 year unit life is the actual purchase justification. Precision optics was supposed to be years away from embodied automation. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/waic-2026-embodied-ai-robots-enter-high-end-optical-module-manufacturing-for-the-first-time-2078345564298125313)

### Research papers worth a look

- **EgoHTR: Egocentric 4D Demonstrations of Human Terrain Traversal (arXiv 2607.13472, submitted July 15; ETH Zurich, Stanford, UC Berkeley, TUM).** An open-sourced reconstruction pipeline capturing 55 scene-aligned 4D human motion sequences (150k+ frames) with egocentric wearables plus a portable 3D scanner, aimed at humanoid locomotion in unstructured terrain. The capture-spec signal: pairing ego sensors with scene geometry at collection time, not annotation time, is becoming the expected format for locomotion data. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13472)
- **Hy-Embodied-VLM-1.0 (Tencent, Hugging Face release, July 15).** Covered in the embedded section above: ~30B MoE embodied VLM with ~3B active parameters per token, built as the perception-reasoning base for Tencent's embodied stack. [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/tencent/Hy-Embodied-VLM-1.0)
- **Cosmos 3 Edge (NVIDIA open release).** A 4B-parameter member of the Cosmos 3 world-foundation-model family, optimized for the Thor architecture. World models sized for 40W edge modules is the deployment-side answer to the WAM inference-cost debate this column has tracked since GigaWorld-Policy-0.5. [NVIDIA](https://blogs.nvidia.com/blog/jetson-thor-robotics-edge-ai-agent/)
