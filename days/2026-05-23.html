    Data collection

    • MobileEgo Anywhere (arXiv 2605.05945) — 200 hours of long-form egocentric data captured on commodity smartphone sensor suites, with an open-source STERA video processing pipeline that turns raw mobile captures into VLA-training-ready format. The “smartphone is good enough” thesis is now an arXiv paper with a dataset behind it. For us this is the bear case: it argues against custom head-mount rigs for a non-trivial slice of the data SOW. Worth reading the failure modes section before our next investor conversation on rig spec. arXiv

    Research papers worth a look

    • MobileEgo Anywhere (arXiv 2605.05945). 200hr egocentric dataset captured on commodity smartphones plus an open STERA pipeline that converts raw phone captures into VLA-ready format. Direct counter-thesis to head-mounted-rig data collection. Read in detail before the next investor / partner pitch on rig spec. arXiv
    • World Action Models: The Next Frontier in Embodied AI (arXiv 2605.12090). New survey on WAMs as unified predictive-state-plus-action foundation models. Good map of where the field is converging. arXiv
    • Physically Native World Models, Hamiltonian perspective (arXiv 2605.00412). Argues for physics-grounded structural priors in generative world models. The other end of the WAM design space from data-only scaling. arXiv
    • Embody4D, generalist 4D world model for embodied AI (arXiv 2605.01799). Video-to-video world model that synthesizes arbitrary novel views from monocular input, with a compositional 4D data pipeline across 30 cross-morphology manipulators. The 4D-vs-2D world model debate now has a real generalist baseline. arXiv
    • Spacetime Optimal-Transport Attention for Visuo-Haptic Imitation Learning (arXiv 2605.20433, May 19). Tri-modal fusion backbone (vision + proprioception + force) plus a diffusion sequence policy. 100% success on tight peg-in-hole vs 93% for cross-attention, gains hold on connector mating and surface-conforming wipe. The latest credible “force feedback matters for VLA” datapoint. Pair with Boston Dynamics’ May 18 Atlas RL writeup. arXiv
    • LeVERB: Humanoid Whole-Body Control with Latent Vision-Language Instruction (arXiv 2506.13751). First vision-language latent-action model for humanoid whole-body control, structured as high-level VL policy plus low-level reactive controller. Read with the Cosmos Reason 2 / GR00T N1.7 stack in mind. arXiv
