### Data collection

- **The RoboSense and GenRobot.AI partnership is the closest thing yet to a sensor maker vertically integrating into the ego-capture business.** Announced July 19 at WAIC: initial work targets multi-view egocentric data and datasets that fuse multiple modalities with 3D representations of physical environments, with sensing hardware wired directly into the full data lifecycle from collection through model training. GenRobot.AI's disclosed footprint, 10,000+ settings across homes, manufacturing, logistics and healthcare and 1M+ hours of human activity, makes it the largest claimed ego corpus operation we have seen named in public. The claim is unaudited, and nothing published says what fraction survives QC. That question is the whole business. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/icv/robosense-genrobotai-team-up-on-real-world-data-for-physical-ai-2078852155371917313)

### Embedded AI systems

- **MiniCPM-Robot is the on-device VLA release of the week.** First drops: MiniCPM-RobotManip, a 1.5B vision-language-action model for generalist manipulation claiming one set of weights across all downstream tasks, and MiniCPM-RobotTrack for embodied target tracking. The proof point was operational, not benchmarked: a venue-guide agent built with Leju Robotics ran fully offline on edge compute, doing real-time Q&A off a local knowledge base while navigating obstacles. A 1.5B VLA that ships usable is a different deployment conversation than a 30B MoE that fits on paper. [Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/waic-2026-modelbest-unveils-embodied-ai-model-series-minicpm-robot-2079096405120483328), [GitHub](https://github.com/OpenBMB/MiniCPM-Robot)

[Gasgoo](https://autonews.gasgoo.com/articles/news/waic-2026-dynaloong-robot-controller-debuts-accelerating-mass-production-of-embodied-intelligence-2079105543602933761)
