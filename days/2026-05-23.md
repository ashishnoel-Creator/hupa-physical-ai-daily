### Data collection

- **MobileEgo Anywhere (arXiv 2605.05945)** — 200 hours of long-form egocentric data captured on commodity smartphone sensor suites, with an open-source STERA video processing pipeline that turns raw mobile captures into VLA-training-ready format. The "smartphone is good enough" thesis is now an arXiv paper with a dataset behind it. For us this is the bear case: it argues against custom head-mount rigs for a non-trivial slice of the data SOW. Worth reading the failure modes section before our next investor conversation on rig spec. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05945)
- **Hyundai actuator stack going US-domestic.** Alongside the 25K Atlas order, Hyundai Mobis is committing to 300,000+ actuators per year manufactured in the US for that fleet. The whole bill of materials for one of the largest humanoid programs in the world is being onshored. If we are pitching capture data to US OEMs in 2027, the supply chain they will be running on is now visible. [Korea Herald](https://www.koreaherald.com/article/10741955)
- **Aria Gen 2 ships to researchers in Q2.** Meta's broad Aria Gen 2 rollout to qualified academic and corporate applicants is now underway, with the Aria Gen 2 Pilot Dataset (cleaning, cooking, eating, playing, walking) live on the project site. Gen 1 install base was 1,000 devices across 300 labs in 27 countries. Gen 2 expands the modality stack and is the most credible head-mounted capture rig outside our own. Worth a careful read of the A2PD schema before we lock our metadata spec. [Meta blog](https://www.meta.com/blog/aria-gen-2-updates/), [A2PD page](https://www.projectaria.com/datasets/gen2pilot/)
- **Apptronik + Mercedes Berlin-Marienfelde Apollo pilot.** Berlin-Marienfelde is now the second Apptronik production site after Spartanburg, running Gemini Robotics + Gemini Robotics Extended Reasoning underneath. Apollo is being used for component transport and assist tasks on the line. The Apptronik data flywheel here is closed-loop: deploy with Mercedes, capture in-factory data, feed Gemini Robotics, redeploy. This is the loop our headcam wedge has to slot into upstream of, not downstream. [WardsAuto](https://www.wardsauto.com/news/mercedes-uses-ai-and-humanoid-robotics-in-production-overhaul/799085/), [Mike Kalil](https://mikekalil.com/blog/mercedes-benz-apptronik-investment/)

### Research papers worth a look

- **MobileEgo Anywhere (arXiv 2605.05945).** 200hr egocentric dataset captured on commodity smartphones plus an open STERA pipeline that converts raw phone captures into VLA-ready format. Direct counter-thesis to head-mounted-rig data collection. Read in detail before the next investor / partner pitch on rig spec. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05945)
- **World Action Models: The Next Frontier in Embodied AI (arXiv 2605.12090).** New survey on WAMs as unified predictive-state-plus-action foundation models. Good map of where the field is converging. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12090)
- **Physically Native World Models, Hamiltonian perspective (arXiv 2605.00412).** Argues for physics-grounded structural priors in generative world models. The other end of the WAM design space from data-only scaling. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00412)
- **Embody4D, generalist 4D world model for embodied AI (arXiv 2605.01799).** Video-to-video world model that synthesizes arbitrary novel views from monocular input, with a compositional 4D data pipeline across 30 cross-morphology manipulators. The 4D-vs-2D world model debate now has a real generalist baseline. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01799)
- **Spacetime Optimal-Transport Attention for Visuo-Haptic Imitation Learning (arXiv 2605.20433, May 19).** Tri-modal fusion backbone (vision + proprioception + force) plus a diffusion sequence policy. 100% success on tight peg-in-hole vs 93% for cross-attention, gains hold on connector mating and surface-conforming wipe. The latest credible "force feedback matters for VLA" datapoint. Pair with Boston Dynamics' May 18 Atlas RL writeup. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20433)
- **LeVERB: Humanoid Whole-Body Control with Latent Vision-Language Instruction (arXiv 2506.13751).** First vision-language latent-action model for humanoid whole-body control, structured as high-level VL policy plus low-level reactive controller. Read with the Cosmos Reason 2 / GR00T N1.7 stack in mind. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.13751)
