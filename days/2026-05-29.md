### Research papers worth a look

- **EgoVerse (arXiv 2604.07607).** Collaborative platform for human data-driven robot learning. 1,362 hours / 80K episodes of human demonstrations across 1,965 tasks, 240 scenes, 2,087 unique demonstrators. The "humans wearing rigs, captured at scale, federated across geographies" thesis with a public number attached. Direct comparable for our captured-data wedge in terms of scale claims. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07607)
- **EgoHumanoid: Unlocking In-the-Wild Loco-Manipulation with Robot-Free Egocentric Demonstration (arXiv 2602.10106).** Egocentric human data as a scalable pathway toward generalizable humanoid control. Robot-free demonstration pipeline. Companion read to EgoExo-WM and HumanEgo in the broader egocentric-video-as-pretraining thesis. The data argument that any third-person or first-person human video becomes training fuel for humanoid loco-manipulation continues to harden through the published literature. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10106)
- **Health-Conditioned Vision-Language-Action Models for Malfunction-Aware Robot Control (arXiv 2605.16056, May 15).** Adds a health-vector input to a VLA backbone, giving the policy information about joint operation angles and torque capability so it can adapt predictions to degraded joints. Worth tracking because every fielded humanoid program will eventually have to ship malfunction-aware control, and most published VLA baselines do not handle this. [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.16056)
