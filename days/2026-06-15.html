    Data collection

    • XRZero-G0 is the collection signal of the window, and it is close enough to our own stack that it is worth reading line by line. X Square Robot’s framework is a wearable rig built on a PICO 4 VR headset with multi-view cameras and two specialized grippers (an H-shaped press-actuated and a G-shaped finger-driven one) that decouple human motion from robot kinematics, with millimeter-accurate 6-DoF pose and edge-side spatiotemporal parsing to sync vision, language and trajectory. That is head-plus-hands egocentric capture with on-device annotation, the same shape as the rig thesis. They open-sourced the hardware designs, the inspection pipelines, the training method and a 2,000-hour dataset on HuggingFace. The capture method is no longer the secret. The validated yield off it is. The Robot Report, XRZero-G0, code on GitHub

    Data annotation & validation

    • XRZero-G0’s “trainability governance” is the annotation thesis written by someone else, and it is the clearest third-party statement of it yet. They run three validation gates. Observation level, where multi-view geometric consistency suppresses visual-kinematic misalignment. Kinematic level, where full-body inverse kinematics with collision and joint-limit constraints filter invalid trajectories. Policy level, where real-robot playback is the final pass criterion. That is QC at capture, QC on the motion, and QC against the downstream model, which is exactly the audit-trailed-yield argument. The fact that a lab built this into an open release tells you the buyers now expect it. Raw hours with no governance layer are getting harder to sell. The Robot Report, XRZero-G0

    Research papers worth a look

    • XRZero-G0: Scalable Robot-Free Data Collection and Cross-Embodiment Transfer for Dexterous Manipulation (arXiv 2604.13001), X Square Robot. The paper behind today’s open release. The headline result is that roughly 10 robot-free human episodes plus 1 real-robot episode matches a pure-robot dataset on evaluated tasks, a claimed up-to-20x cut in real-robot data needs. The mechanism that matters is the closed-loop “collection-inspection-training-evaluation” governance, with three validation gates (multi-view geometric consistency, IK-constrained trajectory filtering, real-robot playback). Read it as the strongest open statement yet that the value in robot-free capture is the inspection layer, not the rig. It is the validation business with a benchmark attached. arXiv 2604.13001
    • The standing June research cluster holds. The world-action-model efficiency wave (Efficient-WAM, Flash-WAM, C3ache, ATM) and the human-to-robot transfer line (LARA, Ego-Pi, the cloth-keypoint synthetic pipeline) are all logged in the Papers DB. XRZero-G0 sits in the same family as Ego-Pi and LARA, all betting that abundant human capture beats scarce robot data, all bottlenecked on the quality of the human capture feeding them. arXiv 2606.08107
